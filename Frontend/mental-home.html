Assessment Modules

Our comprehensive mental health assessment includes these validated screening tools

Health & Vitals

Physical health indicators including blood pressure, heart rate, sleep patterns, and lifestyle factors.

  • Vital signs monitoring
  • Lifestyle assessment
  • Health history review

DASS-21 Scale

Depression, Anxiety and Stress Scale - a comprehensive tool for measuring emotional states.

  • Depression screening
  • Anxiety assessment
  • Stress level evaluation

GAD-7 Assessment

Generalized Anxiety Disorder scale for identifying and measuring anxiety symptoms.

  • Anxiety screening
  • Severity measurement
  • Clinical validation

PHQ-9 Screening

Patient Health Questionnaire for depression screening and severity assessment.

  • Depression screening
  • Severity grading
  • Treatment monitoring
